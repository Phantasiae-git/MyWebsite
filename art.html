About the sizes

The image on the left shows what you get for each size i offer, but note that "head" means a portrait, full of detail, and by comparison the full body won't have a face as detailed for example. Basically everything is proportional. Also, do take into account that i'm much better at drawing faces than bodies



The way my art commissions work is i will draw one character, you can choose from a variety of rendering levels and scopes of the drawing, you DM me on instagram specifying every detail of the commission and if i accept, you pay 50% of the price before i do any work and the rest after i finish. Sketches should take only a day (or a few depending on how busy i am), lineart a bit more, rendered pieces can take several weeks but it all depends on what the commission is specifically so i will give you an individual estimate.
